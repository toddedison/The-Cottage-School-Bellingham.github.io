A little bit about us:

Our program inspires children to discover, experiment, contemplate, create and learn about themselves and their world. At The Cottage School, we cherish the individual and nurture the whole child.

The Cottage School:

  • is located on the Southside.
  • serves children between the ages of three and a half thru six.
  • includes a fully gated, private yard with ample room to run and play.
  • has a small student teacher ratio with a maxium of 14:2.
  • uses environmentally friendly products, recycles everything possible.
  • is in its 12th year running.

Using the practice of Nonviolent Communication we encourage emotional intelligence and respectful relationships.
